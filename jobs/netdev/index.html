.Net Developer

Are you the .NET developer we need?

Who are we looking for?

As a .NET developer, .Net technology is a real passion. You contribute to the succesful development of projects so that our clients are proud on your contribution.

Responsibilities

  • You are a problem-solver
  • You are a convinced user of OO -and design patterns and you are a fan of agile development
  • You are interested in the newest trends and developments
  • You cooperate in our Competence Centers and you share knowledge with your colleagues
  • You are motivated to grow into a .Net architect and you are prepared to obtain the necessary certificates

What are the requirements?

Required Skills

  • You have a Bachelor or Master degree, or equivalent by experience
  • You have more than 2 years work experience in .NET software development
  • Thorough knowledge of OO principles
  • Knowledge of the following technologies / methods is a plus: MVC, Entity Framework, WCF, JQuery, Bootstrap, AngularJs, ..
  • Knowledge of methodologies such as Scrum, Prince2, Test Driven Development are a bonus
  • You have experience / strong interest in mobile and web development
  • You speak Dutch and / or French and English
  • You are flexible, stress-resistant and you like to think outside-the-box

What does Ordina have to offer?

Our .NET department (NCore) is doing more than well. With different beautiful references and a constant stream of challenging projects, we can say that our .NET developers are always giving the best of themselves in their jobs and that we are the best .NET club out there. In addition, we are an open team. You may know us from VISUG or Techorama. We consider it important to invest in a community of professionals and we are not afraid to show it. Internally we are also organized: flat structure, very accessible, lots of room for initiative, thorough distribution of responsibility. Making sure the team is well. Rolled up sleeves. That kind of approach. Just because our .NET club is doing so well, we are looking for new cool colleagues!

  • Challenging assignments and projects for Belgian top companies
  • A dynamic high tech environment with focus on knowledge and innovation
  • An open no-nonsense company culture with room for personal initiative
  • A great number of workshops, trainings and events in cooperation with Ordina Academy
  • Support for obtaining certificates
  • Real possibilities to grow within the company and to clearly define your career on the basis of an Human Talent Management program
  • Regular team events and social company events
  • An attractive salary in accordance to the market situation with a wide remuneration package (company car, laptop, insurance package, meal vouchers,...)
